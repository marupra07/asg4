In this world, I tried to recreate my virtual world but with another twist. I took more time animating my buttons, my lighting controls, and my lighting positions. My cursor now works, so you can move the camera if you press down on the cursor and you can zoom out to see my entire world. I also attempted to create a butterfly, however I struggled to get the dimensions and curves correct. I hope you enjoy my world, and feel free to play around with Cissy the Caterpillar, it follows the cursor movement!
